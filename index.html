Catalina Ruiz is a quirky, outgoing writer and talker who hopes to use her passion for storytelling to give a voice to those who are silenced or overlooked.

She hopes to change the world one story at a time.

By studying public relations at the University of Florida and minoring in international development and humanitarian assistance,

she believes she has the skills to do it.
